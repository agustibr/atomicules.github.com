I'm addicted to tabbed browsing, be it Mozilla or Chimera (Probably even Opera if I had it) I just can't seem to help opening every link I click on in a new tab. It's not like tabbed browsing is new and I don't even think I realise I am doing this. There is obviously no need for this behaviour, but for some reason I seem to feel I must not move forward from the page I'm on (it's like I don't possess a back button), but instead must leave it intact incase there is a link I've left unexplored. I really must stop or get a widescreen monitor to fit all those tabs
